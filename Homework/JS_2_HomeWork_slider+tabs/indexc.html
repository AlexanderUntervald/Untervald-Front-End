Immersive Visual Experience


Our Mobile VR Headset is truly a great VR system. By inserting a smartphone into the front of the viewfinder, you can play in a 360 degree world where the only limit is your imagination. If you’re ready to immerse yourself in a whole new world, get the Mobile Virtual Reality Headset and experience Solas VR.


How to use:


A. [Open front lid]: Pull the switch button at the top of the headset and then open the top cover.


B. [Start app]: Open the installed app client and enter the dual screen right and left interface.


C. [Insert phone]: Insert your phone into the device and adjust the middle line of your phone correctly.


D. [Wear Headset]: Adjust the tension of the 3 bands in the most comfortable way, please use directly with glasses if short-sighted.


E. Should you experience blurring or double vision, adjust the 2 pupil distance adjustment switches at the top of the device to ensure that the observed images are clear and not doubled. The lens will float from left to right and in position with clear view.
